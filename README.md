# Uganda Government Systems Prototype Showcase

This repository was directly inspired by and developed in response to the **Call to Ugandan Innovators: Government Systems Prototype Showcase and National Innovator Registry** issued by the Ministry of ICT and National Guidance (MoICT&NG).

## Details of the Call to Action

The Ministry is seeking to identify and register Ugandan tech innovators capable of building solutions for the digital transformation of government services in the 2025/26 financial year.

* **Issued By:** Ministry of ICT and National Guidance, Republic of Uganda
* **Submission Deadline:** 1 June 2026, 23:59 EAT
* **Submission Portal:** [gdr.ict.go.ug/innovators-showcase](https://gdr.ict.go.ug/innovators-showcase)
* **Core Objectives:**
  1. Identify innovators with capabilities in government digital systems.
  2. Establish the Uganda National Innovator Registry.
  3. Shortlist prototypes for actual deployment or further development.
  4. Promote local content and Ugandan-built digital public infrastructure.

## Prototypes in this Repository

To answer this call, this repository contains a suite of four functional prototypes. Each addresses a specific, high-priority thematic area outlined by the Ministry, demonstrating local capacity to solve systemic government challenges.

### 1. [UgCitizen Resolve Portal](./UgCitizenResolvePortal/)
* **Thematic Area 11: Citizen Engagement & Service Delivery**
* **Focus:** A complaint management and issue-tracking platform bridging the gap between citizens and local government responders.

### 2. [NDP Performance Tracker](./NDPPerformanceTracker/)
* **Thematic Area 12: Monitoring & Evaluation Systems**
* **Focus:** A high-level data visualization dashboard for tracking the performance, budget, and milestones of National Development Plan initiatives.

### 3. [Uganda GovHub API](./UgandaGovHubAPI/)
* **Thematic Area 10: Interoperability & Data Exchange**
* **Focus:** A developer portal and API gateway demonstrating secure, standardized data exchange between isolated Government Ministries, Departments, and Agencies (MDAs).

### 4. [Uganda Land Registry](./UgandaLandRegistry/)
* **Thematic Area 5: Land & Property Administration**
* **Focus:** A digital land titling and verification portal designed to reduce fraud, secure property rights, and streamline land transfers.
